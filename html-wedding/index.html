Our Love Journey

March 2019

First Meeting

We met at a coffee shop downtown. Sarah was reading a book, Michael was working on his laptop. A spilled latte brought us together.

Michael finally gathered the courage to ask Sarah out. We went to the local botanical garden and talked for hours among the roses.

Moving In Together

After a year and a half of dating, we decided to take the next step and move in together. Our first apartment was small but full of love.

August 2023

The Proposal

Michael proposed during our vacation in the mountains, at the exact spot where we had our first kiss. Sarah couldn't stop crying happy tears.

June 2025

Our Wedding Day

Today, we become husband and wife, surrounded by all the people we love most. Our forever starts now.
